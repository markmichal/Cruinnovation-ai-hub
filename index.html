Primary Tool #1
Claude by Anthropic

Think of Claude as that brilliant teammate who never sleeps, never complains, and is always ready to write a first draft at 11pm. We can use it to move faster, write sharper content, and finally stop losing ideas in a tangle of group texts.

Why Use Claude
1
Claude is built for writing and nuance.

Whether it's a devotional, a team update, or a message you've rewritten six times, Claude produces thoughtful, well-crafted writing that sounds like a real person. Because somebody has to.

2
Projects give the whole team a shared AI brain.

Claude Project folders let us store our team's voice, goals, and context in one place. Think of it as onboarding Claude like a new staff member, except Claude actually reads the whole welcome doc. (Ahem, Isaac.)

3
Claude handles sensitive topics with care.

Ministry work requires wisdom and tone, not just words. Claude handles nuance carefully, which makes it a solid partner for faith-based writing and student-facing content. It won't accidentally make things weird.

Primary Tool #2
Gemini by Google

Good news: you already have it. Since Cru uses Google Workspace, Gemini is baked right into Docs, Gmail, Calendar, and more. No new apps, no new passwords. Just open what you're already using and there it is, ready to help.

Why Use Gemini
1
We already have an account through Cru.

No new subscriptions, no new logins, no convincing finance to approve anything. Gemini is already included with our Google Workspace account. The whole team can start today. For free. We love that for us.

2
It's integrated into all our Google products.

Gemini lives right inside Docs, Gmail, and Calendar, which means no switching tabs, no copy-pasting, no "wait, which window had the AI in it?" It's already where you are.

3
Gemini can search the web in real time.

Unlike most AI tools, Gemini can actually search the web in real time. So when you need current info fast, it's not guessing from data it learned two years ago. Progress.

Know Your Tools
Claude vs. Gemini vs. ChatGPT

All three are powerful AI assistants with different strengths. Here's how to think about which to reach for.

Anthropic

Claude

Strong writer, thoughtful reasoner, great for nuanced or sensitive topics. Built for long documents and team collaboration via Projects. Think of it as the workhorse of the group. Our primary team tool this fall.

Best for: Writing & Teamwork
Google

Gemini

Integrates directly with Google Workspace, Docs, Gmail, Slides, and more. Since Cru runs on Google, Gemini is already in your daily tools. No extra login needed.

Best for: Google Workspace
OpenAI

ChatGPT

The most widely known AI. Strong for general Q&A, brainstorming, image creation via DALL-E, and a large plug-in ecosystem. More like a friend you can ask anything. (Bobby.) A great backup and secondary tool.

Best for: General Tasks
