✦ What Gemini can do for you

Draft emails in Gmail instantly

Ask Gemini to write, rewrite, or summarize emails in your Google inbox — saving you time on every message.

Generate content in Google Docs

Stuck on a blank page? Gemini can draft articles, outlines, devotionals, or team updates right inside Docs.

Build slides in Google Slides

Describe a topic and Gemini will suggest structure, bullet points, and speaker notes.

Summarize long documents

Drop in a long report and ask Gemini for a quick summary — great for meeting prep and research.

Answer questions in real time

Organize data in Google Sheets

Ask Gemini to help you format, sort, or analyze data — no spreadsheet expertise required.

Create images with Gemini

Generate original images for presentations, social media, or creative projects directly in the Gemini app.

Brainstorm with your whole team

Use Gemini in shared Docs or Slides so your team can co-create with AI together in real time.

Know Your Tools
Claude vs. Gemini vs. ChatGPT

All three are powerful AI assistants with different strengths. Here's how to think about which to reach for.

Anthropic

Claude

Strong writer, thoughtful reasoner, great for nuanced or sensitive topics. Built for long documents and team collaboration via Projects. Our primary team tool this fall.

Best for: Writing & Teamwork
Google

Gemini

Integrates directly with Google Workspace — Docs, Gmail, Slides, and more. Since Cru runs on Google, Gemini is already in your daily tools. No extra login needed.

Best for: Google Workspace
OpenAI

ChatGPT

The most widely known AI. Strong for general Q&A, coding, image creation via DALL-E, and a large plug-in ecosystem. A great backup and secondary tool.

Best for: General Tasks
